Learn About the Cats!

Here, you can see all the cats and their names! If you hover over each one, they will flip. Upon flipping and seeing the back, you will learn more information about that cat. Please check them all out!

Canelo

About Canelo

Canelo is an orange tabby cat with four white socks (paws). He can be rathe fiesty at times when he's in the mood, but for the most part he is a rather chill cat. He LOVES to play with his brothers!

His favorite treats are Temptations! He will eat them like food and will break into packages and containers just to steal some.

Pugsley

About Pugsley

Pugsley is a fluffy Persian Cat! He is very kind and his personality is as soft as he looks. He is very sweet which is why he is nicknamed "Sweet pea."

He loves to play, but he doesn't get as rough as the others since he is a rather soft guy.

Mickey Blue

About Mickey Blue

His full name is Mickey Blue, but we just call him Blue. Blue is a rather silly guy, he looks like he's always got his mouth open because his lips are so pink! his eyes are always as wide as can be - unless he's sleepy!

He is the biggest trouble maker and always tries to get into things and places he shouldn't be. Other than that, he is very sweet! He makes rather rough biscuits though (be careful!)

Stewart

About Stewart

Stewart once lived outside until we brought him in. He did NOT like being inside and would cry at the door, but he had to stay in since the cold winter was coming. He eventually got along well with the others and now prefers the inside.

Stewart is VERY shy. If he sees a new face, he will hide until he is sure they are gone.

Luna

About Luna

Luna was left behind by her previous owners. We found her and it was decided she would stay here since she grew comfortable and slowly became more loving (she is a little fiesty).

She loves to be pet and will lick! She doesn't like playing with the others and prefers to play alone. She is also the only cat allowed outside because she only sits on the deck to sunbathe, she does NOT want to leave home.

Nico

About Nico

Nico is a little sassy and picky. He's very particular about who is petting him and how. One second he is enjoyin the pets and the next he is annoying. He is still rather sweet though, especially once he finally gets comfortable with you!

I am one of the few lucky people Nico seemed to like the first time he met them!

Hope you are enjoying the cats!
